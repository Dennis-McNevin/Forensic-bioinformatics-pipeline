About the MPS Forensics Pipeline Application

The ANU Bioinfomatics Consultancy developed this forensics bioinformatics pipeline for the National Centre for Forensic Studies, 2016.

The pipeline was jointly funded by the US Defence Forensics Science Centre and the Department of Army Research, Development and Engineering Command (ITC-PAC). It was developed by the National Centre for Forensic Studies at the University of Canberra in collaboration with: Australian National University, Bioinformatics Consultancy; Victoria Police Forensic Services Department (Office of the Chief Forensic Scientist); NSW Forensic and Analytical Science Service; Australian Federal Police (Forensics).
